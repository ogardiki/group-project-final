We are a NGO (non-governmental organization)
that has been saving animals since 2012.
Our aim is to find a safe home to stray animals
in need. We work in collaboration with animal
welfare associations, which provide a temporary
home to our little furry friends. We are glad
to find people interested in saving animals
and willing to adopt a stray.
